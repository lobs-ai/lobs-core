# Research

## Process
1. **Define the question** — what specifically are we trying to learn?
2. **Search broadly** — 2-3 different search queries to get diverse results
3. **Evaluate sources** — prefer official docs, reputable blogs, recent content
4. **Cross-reference** — verify claims across multiple sources
5. **Synthesize** — distill findings into a clear summary

## Output Format
Structure research memos as:
- **Question:** What we investigated
- **TL;DR:** 2-3 sentence answer
- **Key Findings:** Bullet points of important facts
- **Sources:** URLs with brief descriptions
- **Confidence:** High/Medium/Low with reasoning
- **Open Questions:** What we still don't know
